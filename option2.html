Survive the Infection

Player Option 2


You leave your house, you live close to the edge of town and you remember there being a small security base close by. Heading for that, hidden in the night you run in. In the pitch dark room you stagger into a table making a loud noise. The building was seemingly empty however the noise seems to have alerted some nearby guards.


You notice a small cabinet nearby you can either...

OPTION 1: Listen as they walk into the room until they seem distracted enough to try sneak out and away through the way you came in.

OPTION 2: Wait it out silently behind the cabinet as the guards look around.
