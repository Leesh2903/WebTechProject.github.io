Survive the Infection

Home Page

The town you live in has been contaminated with a form of unknown poison, the oxygen and water within the town has become too polluted to sustain life within the area. Nearly everyone in the town has been noted to begin suffering in some way, some have even been discovered dead within their homes. You however are yet to show any form of suffering luckily, perhaps you are not infected yet with the unknown poison. Your town has also been placed within a high security lockdown with groups of armed guards at all possible escape points, you must escape at all cost to try avoiding the infection.


In this game you will be actively making choices via web links to help get your character out from the town and escape, making wrong choices will lead to your characters inevitable death whilst making correct choices will aid in your characters escape. Depending on your choices throughout the game will decide which ending you get.
